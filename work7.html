// HYPNÉTHÆ

* Brand Identity Project

HYPNÉTHÆ is a brand that explores the boundary between what is visible and what remains hidden, using illusion to create an altered perception of reality. Its identity takes shape through an elegant logo built on a serif typeface, designed to convey refinement and visual harmony. A subtle yet distinctive fuchsia accent completes the mark, transforming into a symbol of connection and reinforcing the meaning of the letter “Æ”, becoming a recognizable and unifying brand element.
